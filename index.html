Project Title

Project BRIDGES: Bringing Resources and Inclusive Development through Guided Education in Science

Empowering Out-of-School and At-Risk Youth through Community-Based, Science-Enriched Learning

Problem Statement

To decrease the number of out-of-school youth and support academically at-risk students in Cainta by promoting equal access to inclusive, science-enriched education through a comprehensive outreach and intervention program.

Project Objective

To reduce dropout rates and academic underperformance by proactively bridging gaps in access, awareness, and inclusive, science-enriched education for both out-of-school youth and in-school learners at risk of falling behind.

Root Causes

  • Inaccessible Learning Modalities
  • Limited Emphasis on STEM
  • Weak Community and Institutional Linkages

Project Impact

Educational Access

Increased access to quality education for marginalized youth

STEM Engagement

Enhanced interest and participation in science education

Community Partnerships

Strengthened collaboration between schools and local stakeholders

Academic Performance

Reduced dropout rates and improved student achievement
